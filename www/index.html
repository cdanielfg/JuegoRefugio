FOUNDATION

It is a non-profit organization that seeks to proyect the lives of cats and dogs in a state of abuse and neglect. They provide them with food, medicine and shelter, in order to improve their quality of life and get them a home.






Promote collective awareness through sterilization, respect for life, NO to animal abuse and responsible adoption through 4 simple actions: adopt, sponsor, donate and spread.


SUPPORT US

The foundation goes to great lengths help many animals on a small budget, as they have no official support. So they need your help to ensure the well being and happiness of the animals that live in the foundation.

You can donate supplies from food and medicine to blankets or tiles to adapt the space. you can also spend time with the animals, help with the foundation or make money donations.

You can donate online through PayU safely and quickly by selecting the value you want to contribute. These contributions will be inveted in food, medicine and the adaptation of spaces for pets.
